Apple was Founded

In 1976, there were two individuals with the names of Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak, who came up with the product idea, Apple. The introduction of this new technology put the world in awe with its stunning new visuals and innovations which put new standards to user interactivity and impact the world of new generation phones. By creating new ways of how phones are made this made a breakthrough in modern technology, which helped kickstart this copany and with a massive lead on other competitors. The new uses after bringing the first GUI's into adoption for hand held computers this allowed them to bring new aesthetics to the phone and more personal preferences to the customer in how they wanted their phone to look and act.
